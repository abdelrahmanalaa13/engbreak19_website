The history of Le-Badanek Haq

Le-Badanek Haq 1.0

The first time happened in 2015 at the playground of the faculty of agriculture, Cairo University which included 3 sports activities that were provided for about 155 persons.

2015

Le-Badanek Haq 2.0

The 2nd time of Le-Badanek Haq have taken place in 2016 at Al Jazeera Youth Club, and 7 sports activities were provided for about 550 persons.

2016

Le-Badanek Haq 3.0

We organized Le-Badanek Haq for the 3rd time in 2017 at El-Zamalek Club , which contained 12 sports activities and they were provided to about 650 people.
